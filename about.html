Meet our Team

Jonathan Beavers

Program/Visual Design

Jonathan Beavers is a freelance drill/show designer currently living in Huntsville, Alabama. He attends Athens State University in the Music Industry Communications field where he will receive a degree in Management of Technology and Small Business/ Entrepreneurship.

Jonathan was a member of the Music City Drum Corps in 2011 and also the 2014 Silver Medalist, Bluecoats Drum Corps, where he received the award for being the Visual Performer of the Year. He also was a member of the 7-time World Champions, Music City Mystique’s Indoor Drumline from 2012-2015 winning 2 silver medals, 1 bronze medal and a 4th place finish.

Jonathan is currently the Visual Caption Head of Music City Drum Corps and is also on staff with Music City Mystique during the indoor season. During the school year, he teaches mainly at Bob Jones High School, Fred J. Page High School, and Discovery Middle School. He also helps out and writes drill/ choreography for a few other schools during the marching band and indoor seasons.

Jeremy Smith

Program/Music Design

Jeremy Smith is a composer and arranger from Florence, Alabama. Since beginning his arranging career in 2009, he has been consistently working in the field. Jeremy is an alumni of Muscle Shoals High School, Music City Drum Corps based in Nashville, Tennessee, and Freedom Indoor Percussion based in O'fallontown, Illinois.

As a composer Jeremy has had a great number of successes ranging from premiers to commissions. In 2015 he was the winner of the Shoals Symphony Solo Artist Contest for his work "The Fourth Wall for Symphonic Winds and Percussion". In 2016 he was selected to be a resident at the Azule Artist Residency in Hot Springs, NC where he worked for a month as a resident and composer.

He currently designs, arranges, and instructs for several groups in North Alabama and Middle Tennessee. In 2014 he served as the battery arranger and battery caption head for Southwind Drum and Bugle Corps. He currently serves as a member of the percussion staff at Music City Drum Corps and Clarksville Audio Theatre. He is also serving as a program designer and horn arranger for Conduttore Capolavoro Winds.

Jeremy is currently attending the University of North Alabama where he is finishing a Degree in Instrumental Music with a focus in Music Theory and Composition as well as a minor in Philosophy.

Blake Lankford

Music/Electronic Design

Blake Lankford is a percussionist, teacher, and sound designer in Florence, AL. Blake has been a performing musician for nearly a decade, with various ensembles and touring bands. He was a member of the Music City Mystique Front Ensemble for the 2010 (Bronze), 2011 (Gold), and 2012 (Silver) indoor seasons, and was a member of the University of North Alabama Wind Ensemble for five years during his college career, spending two of those years as the Principal Percussionist and a featured member of the Percussion Ensemble.

Blake has been a member of the education and design staff of several high school programs in the North Alabama and Middle Tennessee area, most recently with Columbia Central High School in Columbia, TN and currently with Siegel High School in Murfreesboro, TN. He also taught with Music City Drum Corps in Nashville, TN from 2013 to 2015, serving as the Front Ensemble Coordinator in 2014/2015 and working with Clarksville Audio Theater in their 2016 indoor season, as well as providing design and consultant services for various other independent marching ensembles in Tennessee, Alabama, and Texas.

Blake’s front ensemble writing, sound design, and program design has been a part of many successful programs in marching band, drum corps, scholastic and independent marching indoor, and concert indoor activities. His is approach is one that uses both acoustic instruments and electronics/effect contribution to create an “environment of sound” that fit and illustrates the mood, context, and desired effect of the show to the ear of the listener.
